Disaster Resource Allocation

This is a simple game that illustrates the idea of resource allocation in disaster respondence. In this game, you will collaborate with programmed agents to . You can choose to become the manager of the agencies detailed below.

Resources

Food: to the injured

Drink: to the injured

Staff: to take care of injured or collect food and drink

Roles

Shelter: You will directly deal with injured

Warehouse: You will control the of food and drink

Station: You will manage the staff and decide on the allocation of them to Shelter or Warehouse

Waiting Room

When you are ready to start, please click the "Ready" button.
